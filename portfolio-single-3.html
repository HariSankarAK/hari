Hospital Management System

Project Date September 2th, 2020
Role Management, Java Application

The Hospital Management System (HMS) is a robust and efficient project that handles patient data and medical records with precision and ease. Using unique patient IDs, the system manages critical information, including addresses, phone numbers, diagnosed diseases, prescribed medications, and ward usage. This streamlined approach ensures that patient data is securely stored and easily retrievable, enabling medical professionals to access past medical history and diagnoses effortlessly when a patient revisits the hospital. By developing the HMS, you have showcased your expertise in data management, database design, and patient information security, providing a practical solution to optimize healthcare processes and enhance patient care.

This project exemplifies the potential of technology to revolutionize healthcare services, facilitating better patient outcomes and improved healthcare delivery.
