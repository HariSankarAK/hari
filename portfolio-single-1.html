College Bus Tracking Mobile App

Project Date July 22th, 2022
Role Tracking, Mobile Application

"Venato" is a college bus tracking system developed using Flutter and Dart for mobile app development, along with MySQL for the database management. The system caters to three types of users: students and faculties who use the bus, admins responsible for managing routes and registering drivers, and drivers who can share their real-time location. With a user-friendly interface and advanced functionalities, "Venato" ensures seamless transportation for college students and staff by enabling them to track bus locations and estimated arrival times.

This project showcases my skills in mobile app development, database integration, and creating practical solutions to address real-world challenges in the college transportation system.
