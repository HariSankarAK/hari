Plant Leaf Disease Detection

Project Date June 4th, 2023
Role Clustering, Website

The Plant Disease Detection System is a cutting-edge project that caters to both farmers and officers in the agricultural sector. By leveraging VGG16, a powerful deep learning model, the system accurately detects diseases in plant leaves based on uploaded images. Farmers can easily upload leaf images and receive prompt identification of the disease, along with recommended remedies. Additionally, the system offers a convenient option for farmers to purchase supplements directly from the supplements page. For officers, the project provides valuable insights by showcasing the diseases detected by each farmer and features a mapping cluster for enhanced visualization and analysis.

Overall, this sophisticated application exemplifies the potential of technology in improving crop management and disease control, demonstrating a strong dedication to advancing the agricultural domain.
