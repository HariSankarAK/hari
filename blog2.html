Yearning Across The Elements

Oh, Gentle wind that wanders far and wide,

Carry my message with a gentle glide,

To my long lost love who's out of reach,

And let her know what I cannot beseech.

Oh, birds that sing with melodies divine,

Share with her the love that is still mine,

And let her hear the echoes of my heart,

That yearns for her and will never depart.

Oh, river that flows with such grace,

Carry my love to her in a peaceful embrace,

And let her know my heart still beats,

With the love for her that forever repeats.

Oh, sun that sets and rises anew,

Illuminate her path and bring into view,

The memories we shared that were divine,

And let them remind her of the love that was mine.

Oh, nature's realm, my faithful friend,

Convey my love until the end,

And tell my love, wherever she may be,

That I miss her dearly, every day and night,

Hoping that our paths may cross again in sight.

-Hari Sankar A K
